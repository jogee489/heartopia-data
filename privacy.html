Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data Collection

Heartopia Hub does not collect or share any personal user data. All game-related data (fish, insects, etc.) is stored locally on your device.

2. Google Drive Backup

If you choose to use the backup/restore feature, the app uses the drive.file scope to interact with your Google Drive. This allows the app to only access files created by Heartopia Hub for the purpose of backing up your local database. We do not see your photos, documents, or other Drive files.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Play Services for authentication. Their privacy policy can be found at Google Privacy & Terms.
